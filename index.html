Open Tracing

Based on Google's dapper paper and full OpenTracing compliant, Haystack has distributed tracing at its core. Designed to ingest large volume of production traces with high resiliency and scalability.

Designed for Extensibility

Haystack is designed to be easily enhanceable. Built around a Kafka backbone, its easy to spin off a new streams app for building your own Haystack subsystem.

Why stop at Tracing?

Why just stop at distributed tracing when you can leverage the opentracing data to create operation trends, setup anomaly detection and build service dependency graphs.
